Frequently Asked Questions

How do I restore my Pro purchase?

Go to Settings > Upgrade to Pro > Restore Purchases. Your lifetime purchase is tied to your Apple ID and can be restored on any device.

Is there a subscription?

No. Spicy Focus Pro is a one-time $4.99 lifetime purchase. No subscriptions, ever.

What's the free trial?

All Pro features are unlocked free for 7 days after you first install the app. No credit card required, no auto-renewal.

Why ambient noise?

A 2024 meta-analysis found that ambient noise has a statistically significant benefit for ADHD focus. Brown noise is the community favorite. You can read more in the app under Settings > The Science.

Does the app collect my data?

No. All your data stays on your device. We have no servers, no analytics, no tracking. Read our full Privacy Policy.
